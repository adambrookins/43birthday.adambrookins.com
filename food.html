Oaxaca Old Fashioned

1.5 oz reposado + 0.5 oz mezcal, barspoon of agave, 2 dashes Angostura, big cube, orange peel flamed over the top. The deepest drink on the menu.

Slow sipper

The house pour

The Kirkland margarita

No shame, all flavor. This is the workhorse pitcher of the party.

Pitcher recipe — serves ~8

Costco run: one bottle of Kirkland Silver, limes, and you're most of the way there.
  • 2 cups Kirkland Signature Silver Tequila
  • 1 cup fresh-squeezed lime juice (10–12 limes — fresh matters, this is the whole trick)
  • ¾ cup orange liqueur (Kirkland's triple sec is fine, Cointreau if fancy)
  • ½ cup agave nectar, adjusted to taste
  • 1 big pinch of salt, straight into the pitcher
  • Rim half salt, half Tajín — do a tray of glasses ahead of time
Stir with plenty of ice, taste, and add a splash of water if it bites. Float 0.5 oz of mezcal on top of any glass to upgrade it to the "birthday boy version."
